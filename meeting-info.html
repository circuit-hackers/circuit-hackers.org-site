Join Us at a Meeting, Become a Club Member

Welcome!

We would be happy to have you visit one of our meetings. We'd love to have more club members - our club is best suited for ages 10 through 17, but interest and aptitude count most!

We meet most Mondays at 4:45 PM at the University of Maine Cooperative Extension at 138 Pleasant Street in Farmington, Maine.

For more information about the club, please view this introduction and information document.

If you are a prospective new member, please fill-out the questionnaire at the end of the above-linked document, and bring it to the meeting.

The extension office's front door is on Pleasant street, but please enter through the back door, directly into our meeting room!



You can contact the extension office at: (207) 778-4650.

We are a 4-H technology and engineering club working through the University of Maine Cooperative Extension in Franklin County.
